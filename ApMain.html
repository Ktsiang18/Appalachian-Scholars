Appalacian Scholar Program
Our future is in education.
1
We will provide the students with a broad perspective on the college application process and how to fund their education regardless of socioeconomic status
2
We will spend 4 weeks with our students in a classroom setting, to improve college reading and writing skills, in order to provide them with the best possible tools to thrive at our nation's top colleges.
3
We will provide our students with remote mentorship to ensure they review their applications and meet hard deadlines, followed by a personal sponsors to assist them as they integrate with their respective campuses.

Our Vision

Our goal is to bring our brightest students to the best universities around the country and set them up for success on day one of their freshman year.

Program Information:

  • Duration: June 5-June 30, 2017
  • Location: University of Charleston

Qualifcations:

  • Current juniors and rising seniors are eligible to apply
  • Must have a cumulative GPA of 3.5 and above
  • Must have a PSAT score of 1270 or ACT score of 26 and above
  • Applications are accepted on a rolling basis until June 1st
